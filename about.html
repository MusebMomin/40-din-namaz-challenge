About the Challenge

Mission

The 40 Day Namaz Challenge is designed to help Muslims build a consistent habit of performing all five daily prayers on time. Our mission is to create a supportive community that encourages spiritual growth and discipline.

Why 40 Days?

Studies show that it takes approximately 40 days to form a habit. By committing to 40 days of consistent prayer, participants can establish a lasting spiritual routine that continues beyond the challenge period.

How It Works

  • Register for the challenge with your details
  • Choose your start date
  • Track your daily prayers using our progress tracker
  • Receive daily reminders and motivation
  • Complete all 5 prayers for 40 consecutive days
  • Celebrate your achievement!
